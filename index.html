Often, we don't have access to professional counselling, or we are afraid to access it. Often, we would rather confide in a girlfriend, sister or mentor in a safe place without judgement or serious consequences while we try to make sense of our lives and come to a decision.
The UR Pathway and tools can be accessed on your time, in private, no matter what your situation is. UR aims to help you make sense of your life and relationships, starting with the one you have with yourself. UR focuses on the change that comes from within.

UNLIMITED REVISIONS - Exposing The Glitch

The book is the story of my early experience with abuse and the long-lasting impact on my future relationship choices. Many will recognize the signs I missed. Journalizing helped me identify my role, take back control and find the courage to stand up for myself and my children. The book is also a guide with lessons for other women and the family and friends supporting them.

Your story is unique, but the pathway you are on is well-traveled. Your answers will guide you towards a better, safer future.
